Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Introduction

Sacred Pause is designed to be calm, simple, and respectful of your privacy. This policy explains what information is handled by the app today. The experience is primarily local-first, meaning most of your data stays on your device.

Information we store

Sacred Pause stores certain preferences on your device so the app can function as intended. This may include your selected apps, prayer timing, reminders, and other settings you configure. We do not require an account, and we do not maintain a central user database tied to your identity for everyday use.

Screen Time permissions

If you choose to enable Screen Time features, Sacred Pause uses Apple’s system frameworks to apply app restrictions based on your selections. These permissions are handled by iOS and use privacy-protecting tokens.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed and managed by Apple through your Apple ID. Sacred Pause does not receive your full payment information.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Sacred Pause may send reminders based on the schedule you set.

Data sharing

Sacred Pause does not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as Sacred Pause evolves.
